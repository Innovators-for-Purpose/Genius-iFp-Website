Unlocking the Innovator Within

The Genius Within is a new kind of monument—one that redefines how we honor innovation. Too often, honors and tributes have overlooked the contributions of many. This installation makes that genius visible, celebrating those whose impact has gone underrecognized. But it does more than commemorate our honorees. With mirrors woven into its design, Genius Within invites everyone to see themselves in the story of innovation—reminding us that genius is not limited to a few, but alive in all of us.
